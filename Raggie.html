Raggiecoin ($RAG)

RaggieCoins are the official currency of our ecosystem. Holders of CryptoRaggies NFTs can participate in our Play-to-Earn games to earn $RAG, which can then be swapped for ADA or supplied to liquidity pools on DEXes. $RAG can also be used to mint in-game items, powerful upgrades and special edition NFTs in RaggieStore! RaggieCoin is a fixed-supply token and has largely been a fair-launch token with no ISO, IDO or ICOs!
